Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Space Tetris ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data (if you sign in with Apple): We receive an identifier and, if you allow it, your name and email from Apple. We use this to create and manage your account and to sync your nickname and preferences.
  • Game data: When you are signed in, we store your nickname, score, and leaderboard entries on our servers so we can show rankings and restore your progress.
  • Premium and purchases: We store your premium subscription status and rocket purchase history to provide the correct features (e.g. ad-free experience, monthly rockets). Purchase processing is handled by Apple; we do not store your payment details.

2. How we use your data

We use the data above to run the app, show the leaderboard, personalize your experience, and provide support. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Advertising

If you are not a premium subscriber, we show ads in the app (banner and full-screen) via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device and usage data for ad personalization. You can learn more in Google's Privacy Policy. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

4. Data storage and security

Account and game data are stored on our servers and are protected with industry-standard measures. We keep data for as long as your account exists or as required by law.

5. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us. You can also delete your account from within the app (Settings). If you are in the EEA or UK, you have additional rights under applicable data protection laws.

6. Children

Space Tetris is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have done so, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at the support address provided in the app or on the App Store listing.
